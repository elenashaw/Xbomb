## fin equation

### basic assumptions

* One-dimensional,
* steady-state conduction in straight fin of uniform cross section
*  no internal energy generation

### extended assumptions

* fin base temperature is constant
* tip is adiabatic(尖端不考虑对流传热)
* 四周：对流换热
